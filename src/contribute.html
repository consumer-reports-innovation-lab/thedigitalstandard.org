Help Build
The Digital Standard

The Digital Standard is published openly for anyone to use. It is the framework for independent testing and evaluation by Consumer Reports and many others.

The Digital Standard is maintained by a coalition of researchers, developers and advocates who share a common belief: human interests should be at the core of consumer technologies. We will continue to shape, refine, test and improve the standard in order to ensure it is comprehensive, effective, and up-to-date in a rapidly-changing marketplace.

We are seeking individuals and organizations who can support this work in the following roles:

Strategic collaborators to work closely with our teams to evolve The Digital Standard

Independent testers and testing labs with the capabilities to evaluate products against The Digital Standard

Reviewers who evaluate and provide feedback on documented procedures & methodologies

Consumer rights, media and policy advocates to amplify results
