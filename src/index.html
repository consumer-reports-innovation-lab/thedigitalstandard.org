The

Raising the standard for connected products, services and systems.

The Digital Standard is a framework for testing organizations, like Consumer Reports, to evaluate consumer technologies and how they respect people’s interests and needs.

The Digital Standard is built openly, as a consensus among the tech community. It is used broadly by independent testing organizations, researchers and product teams.

View The Standard

The Digital Standard helps companies stand out by building products that prioritize user values, starting with:

Security
Privacy
Ownership
Governance

Get Involved

The Digital Standard framework is used by independent testing organizations all over the world. It evolves to address a constantly changing digital marketplace. We invite you to join a broad community of researchers, testers and technologists to expand the framework by contributing improvements and new testing methodologies.
